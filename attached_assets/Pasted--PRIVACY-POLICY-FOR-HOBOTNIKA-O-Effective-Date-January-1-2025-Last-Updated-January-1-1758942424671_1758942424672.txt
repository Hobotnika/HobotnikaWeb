## 6. Cookies and tracking technologies

### 6.1 Cookie Categories
We use the following categories of cookies:

**Strictly Necessary Cookies**
Essential for website functionality, security, and fraud prevention. These cannot be disabled.

**Functional Cookies**
Remember your preferences and enhance website features. Consent required in EU/UK.

**Analytics Cookies**
Help us understand website usage through Google Analytics. We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization and have configured it to comply with privacy regulations. Consent required in EU/UK.

**Advertising/Marketing Cookies**
Enable targeted advertising through Google Ads, including conversion tracking and remarketing. Used to measure advertising effectiveness and deliver relevant ads. Consent required.

### 6.2 Google Services Disclosures

**Google Analytics**
We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect and process data. We have implemented IP anonymization, disabled data sharing, and configured appropriate retention periods. Learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use their services at www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/

**Google Ads and Remarketing**
We use Google Ads for advertising and remarketing services. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

**Google Consent Mode v2**
For visitors from the EEA and UK, we implement Google Consent Mode v2, which adjusts how Google tags behave based on consent status for:
- ad_storage: Controls storage of advertising-related data
- analytics_storage: Controls storage of analytics-related data
- ad_user_data: Controls collection of user data for advertising
- ad_personalization: Controls personalized advertising

### 6.3 Managing Cookies
**Opt-Out Options:**
- Google Ads Settings: Visit www.google.com/settings/ads to opt out of personalized advertising
- Network Advertising Initiative: Visit www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
- Your Online Choices: Visit www.youronlinechoices.eu (EU) or www.aboutads.info/choices (US)
- Google Analytics Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
- Browser Settings: Configure your browser to refuse cookies (may impact website functionality)
- Global Privacy Control: We honor GPC signals where legally required
