## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach** - Drawing inspiration from professional B2B consultancy websites like HubSpot and Salesforce, adapted for Estonian business culture with unique octopus-themed brand elements.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Navy Blue: 214 85% 32% (brand primary)
- Secondary Blue: 217 91% 60% (interactive elements)
- Accent Cyan: 199 89% 64% (highlights and CTAs)
- White: 0 0% 100% (backgrounds)
- Light Gray: 220 13% 91% (subtle backgrounds)
- Dark Gray: 215 25% 27% (text)

### B. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, professional sans-serif
- **Headings:** Font weights 600-700, sizes from 2.5rem (h1) to 1.125rem (h6)
- **Body Text:** Font weight 400, 1rem base size with 1.6 line height
- **Accent Text:** Font weight 500 for emphasis

### C. Layout System
**Spacing Units:** Consistent use of Tailwind units 4, 8, 12, and 16 for margins, padding, and gaps. Sections use py-16 or py-20 for vertical spacing.

### D. Component Library

**Navigation:**
- Fixed header with subtle shadow
- Logo placeholder on left, horizontal menu on right
- Mobile hamburger menu with slide-out drawer
- Navy blue background with white text

**Hero Section:**
- Full viewport height with gradient overlay
- Large typography hierarchy
- Two-column layout: compelling headline left, octopus-inspired visual right
- Primary and secondary CTAs

**Content Sections:**
- Alternating layouts with generous whitespace
- Card-based components with subtle shadows
- Service cards in 3-column grid on desktop
- Testimonial components with client logos

**Forms:**
- Clean, minimal styling with focus states
- Consistent input heights and border radius
- Cyan accent for active states

### E. Estonian Business Professionalism
- Conservative layout structure respecting local business expectations
- Professional imagery and clean aesthetics
- Subtle octopus motifs integrated tastefully without overwhelming corporate credibility
- Estonian language considerations in typography and spacing

## Images
**Hero Background:** Abstract octopus tentacle patterns or geometric interpretations - sophisticated and subtle, not literal cartoon octopus
**Service Icons:** Custom octopus-inspired icons representing adaptability, intelligence, multi-tasking
**About Section:** Professional team photos, Estonian business environment imagery
**Case Studies:** Client work examples, analytics dashboards, growth charts

The website features a **large hero image** as the primary visual anchor, establishing brand identity immediately upon landing.

## Key Principles
1. **Intelligent Adaptability:** Responsive design that works seamlessly across devices
2. **Professional Trust:** Clean layouts with plenty of whitespace
3. **Estonian Heritage:** Subtle nods to local business culture
4. **Octopus Metaphor:** Multi-armed approach to digital marketing solutions
5. **Conversion Focus:** Clear CTAs and logical user journey toward contact
